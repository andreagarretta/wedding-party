Getting to Osaby

How to
get there

🚂

From Copenhagen

  • 1
    Train from København H to Växjö C Station — approx. 2h 20min via Öresundståg / SJ.
  • 2
    From Växjö: 13 minutes by car to Osaby Säteri.
  • 3
    We can organise shared lifts from the station — or rent a car in Växjö (Europcar, Hertz available).
✈️

From Italy & Argentina

  • 1
    Fly to Copenhagen (CPH) or Malmö (MMX) — many connections from Rome, Milan and Buenos Aires.
  • 2
    From the airport, take the direct train to København H and follow the route above.
  • 3
    Alternatively: direct flight to Växjö Kronoberg Airport (VXO) if a connection is available.
🚗

Coordinating arrivals

We'll set up a group to coordinate train times and share car rides from Växjö. If you're arriving by train, let us know and we'll sort the transfer. If you prefer freedom, rent a car and enjoy the Swedish countryside on your own terms.
